How Shram works

Finds it. Drafts it. Does it.

No setup. No integrations. No new inbox to check. Shram runs in the background across the tools you already use.

1
Finds what you missed
Shram watches your Gmail, Google Calendar, and WhatsApp — the places where follow-ups actually live. It spots when a conversation needs a response, a check-in, or a next meeting, before the window closes.
Passive · No setup needed
2
Drafts with full context
Using an on-device memory layer that retains your conversation history, Shram drafts a reply that sounds like you — referencing what was actually discussed, not a generic template. If you promised a revised proposal two months ago, Shram remembers.
Context-aware · On-device memory
3
Executes in one click
The follow-up appears as a task. You review it, hit execute, and Shram's desktop agent sends it — across whatever app the conversation was in. You stay in control. Shram does the work.
One-click execution · Desktop agent
